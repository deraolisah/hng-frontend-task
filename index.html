My 2-Year Vision
(In Tech)

As a FullStack Web Developer, I get teary-eyed whenever I look back on how far I've come since I decided to pursue a career in Tech, in 2019... I've always been fascinated by the rapid pace of innovation and the endless opportunities it presents. And looking onwards to the coming years, I'm excited to see what I become, which is why I have to set ambitious goals that will help me grow both personally and professionally in the tech industry. Here's what I aim to achieve:

Short-term goals (next 6-12 months)

  • Level up my skills: I want to dive deeper into Computer Networking and Cyber Security, so I'll take online courses and attend workshops to improve my skills. To achieve this, I will dedicate 2 hours every week to online courses, tutorials, and coding challenges. In order to stay current with industry trends.

  • Build my professional network: I'll attend at least 3 industry conferences and meetups to connect with like-minded professionals and learn from their experiences.

  • Take on new challenges: I'll volunteer for a high-visibility project at work that will help me demonstrate my skills and take on more responsibilities. I'll seek out a mentor who can provide guidance and support as I navigate my career.

Long-term goals (next 1-2 years)

  • Advance my career: I aim to get promoted to a senior developer role within the next 18 months, where I can lead projects and mentor junior developers. I'll explore opportunities to transition into a product management role, where I can combine my technical skills with business acumen.

  • Pursue my passion project: I'll dedicate at least 10 hours a week to developing my own startup idea, which focuses on using AI to improve healthcare outcomes. I'll build a minimum viable product (MVP) and gather feedback from potential customers to refine my idea.

  • Establish myself as a thought leader: I'll write at least 6 articles or blog posts on topics like AI, data science, and cybersecurity, and publish them on platforms like Medium or LinkedIn. I'll speak at least twice at industry events or conferences, sharing my expertise and building my professional brand.

Personal goals

  • Find a better work-life balance: I'll prioritize self-care by exercising at least 3 times a week and taking breaks throughout the day to recharge. I'll set aside dedicated time for my loved ones and prioritize activities that bring me joy and fulfillment.

  • Give back to the community: I'll volunteer at least 10 hours a quarter to help underrepresented groups in tech, whether it's through mentoring or teaching coding classes.

Action plan

  • To achieve these goals: I'll create a schedule and stick to it, dedicating time each week to learning, networking, and working on my passion project. Hold myself accountable by tracking my progress and celebrating my wins. Be open to feedback and willing to pivot when necessary, knowing that growth and learning are lifelong processes. By setting these goals and working towards them, I'm excited to see the progress I'll make in the next two years and the impact I'll have on the tech industry.
